The Guest network is for visitors at the NTNU campus who are not registered students, Teachers, professors or workers at the university.





Eduroam is an internet service for students and Teachers at NTNU


What is Eduroam?

Eduroam is an international Internet service offered to students
and Teachers at universities across the world. The service ensures
that its users has Access to the internet at a multitude of campuses
in different countries as long as the university itself has adopted
the eduroam system. In addition to free internet Connection at
different campuses, Eduroam provides free Access at airports
in most countries.


How to connect to Eduroam

1. Download and run relevant eduroam connection file
2. Log in with: username@ntnu.no as your username.
3. Click "install" to Complete the installation.
4. If the Connection has not been established, redo previous steps as
username or password might have been wrong
